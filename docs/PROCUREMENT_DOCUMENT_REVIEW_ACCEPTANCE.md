# Procurement Document Review Acceptance

- Open purchase requests and purchase orders before approval.
- Show requester, project, supplier, required dates, items, quantities, prices, tax, discounts, and totals.
- Show approval and rejection history with reasons.
- Prevent deletion after approval, receipt, invoice, or cost posting.
- Preserve received quantities and financial history.
- Provide print/PDF-ready document views and later reuse them for professional exports.
- Review additive migrations before applying them.
- Run rollback-safe smoke tests, full tests/build, Vercel, and cross-module regression before merge.
